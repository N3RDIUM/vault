A processor is anything that performs the two tasks: (1) direct the processing of information and (2) process the actual information, both in response to a computer program.

Other things that facilitate the usage of a processor or provide certain capabilities (peripherals, network cards, memory, etc) together with the processor is what we call a “computer system”, or just “computer” nowadays.
